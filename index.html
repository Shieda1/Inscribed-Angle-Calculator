What is the nscribed angle?
The inscribed angle is the angle formed in the interior of a circle when two chords intersect on the circle. In simple words it is the angle subtended at a point in the circle by two given points on the circle.
How to calculate nscribed angle?
To calculate nscribed angle you should use the following formula:
inscribed angle = 1/2 * intercepted arc
Why use nscribed angle?
The inscribed angle is used in order to find the intercepted arc.
